Welcome to a Center of Inclusive Excellence

At Sabeelus Salaam Inclusive Academy, we believe that every child is a unique amanah (trust) placed in our care. Our mission goes beyond academic grades; we strive to mold character, instill perseverance, and build a foundation of moral integrity.

Located here in Dulla Tafa, we are proud to offer a curriculum that competes globally while remaining rooted in our local values. We invite you to join our journey.

About Our School

Our History

Established to bridge the gap in quality education in Kagarko LGA, SSIA started as a humble initiative. Over the years, through the dedication of our staff and the support of the Dulla Tafa community, we have grown into a full-fledged academy serving Nursery, Primary, and Secondary students.

Our Vision

To be a leading inclusive institution that nurtures future leaders with moral integrity and academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and perseverance in every student.
